Description of Classes

  • Yoga Class

    This class is designed for all experience levels. Students can expect a yoga practice focused on relaxation and flexibility. Benefits include reduced stress and increased flexibility.

    Instructor: Joana Silva (yoga certified by Yoga Alliance).
    Level: All levels.
    Requirements: Yoga mat.

  • Boxing Class

    This class is designed for beginners and intermediate students. Students can expect boxing practice focused on technique and physical conditioning. Benefits include improved hand-eye coordination and increased cardiovascular endurance.

    Instructor: Pedro Santos (former professional boxer).
    Level: Beginner/Intermediate.
    Requirements: Boxing gloves.
